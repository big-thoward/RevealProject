{{'How to Buy' | r | xlat}}

  1. Browse the product catalog via the product categories.

  2. Add product(s) to cart.

  3. Continue to shop, or proceed to checkout.

  4. Enter in your shipping address or choose from a pre-existing shipping address.

  5. Choose your desired shipping method.

  6. Enter in your billing address or choose form a pre-existing billing address.

  7. Enter in your credit card information.

  8. Click the Submit Order button. You will receive an order email confirmation.
