Shipping Information

  • All orders will ship via USPS within 14 days of the close of the Project.
  • Shipping charges are based on the total weight of your order and the location the order is being shipped to.
  • Shipping charges will appear on the cart page prior to submitting your order.

Orders will ship via the following time frame based on location:

  • Orders to US Addresses will arrive within 2-4 business days of shipping
  • Orders to all other addresses will arrive in 4-10 business days of shipping

All Reveal Project items are offered as limited time custom products. Based on order volume, orders may be released to production prior to the Project close date. Once your order has moved to production you will receive a notification email with tracking information. Your order will be in production for approximately 7 to 10 business days before shipping. Once your order has shipped your tracking information will be updated.

If you have any questions about your order or if you feel your order is overdue please contact us and provide your order number and contact information.

How To Buy

  1. Browse the product page to choose your product.

  2. Add product(s) to cart.

  3. Continue to shop, or proceed to checkout.

  4. Enter in your shipping address.

  5. Choose your desired shipping method.

  6. Enter in your billing address or choose from a pre-existing billing address.

  7. Enter in your credit card information.

  8. Click the Submit Order button. You will receive an order email confirmation.
